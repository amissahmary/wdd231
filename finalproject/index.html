Project Site Plan

Site Name: The Levite generation

The Levite Generation is a community-based organization dedicated to empowering young people and fostering their spiritual and personal growth. I chose this name because that is the name of the group and hence it will be easier for people to find it.

Site Purpose

The site provides information about the group, when it was established and what motivates them to do what they do. Their, mission and vision statements. Their upcoming events and how to be a part of it. Records of their previous ministrations and the impacts they have made.

Scenarios

Here are some typical scenarios that users might encounter while navigating the site:

  1. What are the organization's mission and values?
  2. When and where is their next ministration?
  3. How can i become a member of this group?
  4. How are their ministrations like?

Color Scheme

The color scheme for the site will be a combination of yellow and white, with accents of red and black.

Typography

The typography for the site will be clean and modern, using a sans-serif font for headings and a serif font for body text. The font sizes will be appropriately scaled to ensure readability and visual hierarchy.
