About Us

The Levite Generation is a creative worship ministry based in Miotso, Ghana. We gather young people to worship God, to tell His story, and to honor Him through movement, poetry, and drama. A worship ministry that leads people to Christ through dance, spoken word, and drama.

We believe the arts can open hearts, ignite faith, and bring communities together in joyful praise. Our ministry uses expressive arts to make the gospel relevant and accessible, inviting every visitor into a deeper encounter with Christ.

What We Do

  • Dance Ministry: We worship through choreography that points people to God and inspires spiritual encounter.
  • Spoken Word: We declare scripture and testimony through powerful, poetic performance.
  • Drama: We bring biblical stories and life lessons alive with compelling stage ministry.

Our Mission

To lead people into a life-changing relationship with Jesus through dynamic worship arts, training young believers to serve with excellence, humility, and bold faith.

Our Vision

To raise a generation transformed by the gospel and empowered to carry Christ's light into their families, schools, and communities through Spirit-led dance, spoken word, and drama.
